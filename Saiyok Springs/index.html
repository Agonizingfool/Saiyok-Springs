SAIYOK SPRINGS

Our Water


Sai Yok Springs by Radach Family & Co. Organics is Thailand’s first closed-loop, locally-sourced premium mineral water brand. Our search for the purest and most delicious water source in Thailand led us to the western province of Kanchanaburi. Deep in the Sai Yok Valley, between two national parks, lies an untouched oasis of natural spring water that is fresh, crisp and palatable to the tongue. The natural filtration through hundreds of meters of limestone in Erawan National Park produces a water that is not only pristine, but naturally high in calcium and low in nitrate

Sustainability

Transportation

The ecological barrier of transporting water thousands of kilometers in single-use containers leaves a significant footprint on the environment.

Energy

Our bottling facility is 80% solar-powered, with the remaining energy being sourced from Kanchanaburi’s hydropower system

Packaging Reuse

Our closed-loop packaging is reusable up to 200 times.

Avoidance of Plastics

Reusable glass bottles (after 3 cycles), produce 85% less emissions than single-use glass bottles and 70% less than single PET

Local Impact

A revenue share is given to fund the local school and temple, provide organic farming assistance, and help with English education for the village.

Doing Good

Our local community’s lifestyle in tandem with nature and organic farming techniques ensures that each drop has never been exposed to toxins from industrial agriculture.


  • For this reason, it is our desire to reinforce and give back to the organic farming models in the community and to improve the villagers’ quality of living.
  • A portion of our revenue is diverted back to the local community to help fund the development of the agricultural & educational systems in Sai Yok – marketing their organic products, improving the local school and creating a more robust organic farming infrastructure.
  • Easy to maintain drinking water systems will be installed in Phu Ongka and surrounding communities to provide free and healthy drinking water for all inhabitants
  • We support re-forestation (mangroves) and water conservation projects across Thailand
